Set in the picturesque seaside village of Clifden, McDello's Bar & Grill hits the spot for casual dining, a lively night out with friends, after work cocktails or just wasting the weekend away! You can enjoy fabulous sunset views from the patio deck, or stay until late on Saturday's with a live DJ in the Function Room! Set up by two college friends in 2019, McDello's has experienced thriving business and will be opening a secong Bar & Grill in early 2020.
